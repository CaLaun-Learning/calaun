About Calc Tutor

Calc Tutor is a free, open-source educational tool designed to help students learn calculus by showing step-by-step solutions. Whether you're working through derivatives, integrals, or limits, we break down each problem so you can understand the "why" behind every step.

This is a non-profit project with a simple mission: quality math education should be accessible to everyone, everywhere. No paywalls, no premium tiers, no ads. Just free calculus help whenever you need it.
